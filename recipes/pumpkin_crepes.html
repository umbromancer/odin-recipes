Pumpkin Crepes with Fontina Cheese Sauce

Description

An elegant crepe with a comforting cheesiness that everyone will love. Diced pumpkin and leeks combine for a smooth, buttery filling while shredded fontina is baked until melted on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 1/2 ounces all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Beat egg with flour and salt
  2. Cook crepes
  3. Cook pumpkins
  4. Make fontina sauce
  5. Pre-heat oven
  6. Fill and fold crepes
  7. Bake crepes in oven
