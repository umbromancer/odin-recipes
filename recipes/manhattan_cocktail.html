Manhattan Cocktail

Description

The Manhattan, a mixture of whiskey, vermouth, and bitters, was created in New York sometime in the late 19th century. Printed records of the cocktail's recipe date back to the 1890s.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
  • Â½ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Add whiskey, vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass. Add ice and stir until chilled
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a maraschino cherry
  3. Enjoy!
